MONO-DUO (2022)

Mono-Duo is made with the monochromatic coloring technique. This technique involves having to draw everything in greyscale first and then use a gradient color scheme to color the entire artwork. Coloring in greyscale first is a good habit to get into as an artist, so I used this project as an opportunity to practice.

Mediums used:
Photoshop
