CLIMB (2021)

Climb is a short film based on my original concept called Soar. It follows the character Sou that goes on a journey to the peak. The story is based on an itch.io game I created where the goal was to reach the top. I transformed my original game idea into a hand-drawn 2D frame-by-frame animation.

Mediums used:
Photoshop & Premiere Pro

Music by:
Takahiro Obata - Isabella's Lullaby
