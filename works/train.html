TRAIN TO NOWHERE (2022)

Train to Nowhere is a 2D/3D satisfying loop animation that emulates lo-fi vibes. My inspirations were my numerous public transport trips, the music that accompanied me along the way and my fondness for Japanese culture. The environment is made in 3D, whereas the character is hand-drawn and animated in 2D.

Mediums:
Blender, Photoshop & Premiere Pro

Music by:
Jhove x Dillan Witherow - when I close my eyes
