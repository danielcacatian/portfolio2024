Daniel Cacatian is an English and French-speaking 2D/3D artist that operates in Montréal, Canada. He has Bachelor in Fine Arts for Computation Arts from Concordia University, graduated as an honor student with a college degree in Graphic Design from Vanier College and earned an Art Méritas in high school. This recognition led him to receive various commissions, such as creating logos for acquaintances, 2D art for Twitch Streamers and interning as a graphic designer for McGill University. Daniel Cacatian continues to sharpen his skills as an artist so he can take them into the film and video game industry.
